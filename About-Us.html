Our Main Goals

We at Papua National School have many goals for our students and our school, firstly, we'd like to create a safe, comfortable and enjoyable learning environment, second we'd like to fully realize a learning process that is able to provide provisions to students including knowledge, skills and positive behavior including respect, gratitude/attitude of gratitude, caring, humility, discipline, responsibility and broad insight, third, we'd like our students to master the basics of cognitive and technology and apply them to continue to a higher level, fourth, we hope to realize an optimal learning achievement and produce graduates with good distinction, fifth, we'd like to develop and channel our students' abilities and talents in the academic field through intracurricular and extracurricular activities, and finally, we hope to become a model school for the development and progress of education in Papua.

    Bulan Bahasa

    During the end of every October, our school has a special event and competition relating to the three languages our students learn, Indonesian, English, and Mandarin.
