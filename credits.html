Background

During our free time, we usually talk a lot about school, life, and other miscellaneous topics. One of these topics we were talking about was about our school, and what we'd do to improve it. The first idea we had was advertising as this school needed more students. We realized the easiest way we could do this was by creating a website to promote it. After that, we started brainstorming ideas during our breaks.

Our Goal

It was during one our classes where we were talking with one of our teachers after we had finished our schoolwork earlier. We decided to ask why there wasn't a website to promote this school, to which they replied with confusion since the school had never had a website in all their years of teaching in it. So at that moment, we agreed upon building our own. With the main goal for us being to attract more students in our class, making the school more lively.

Creation Process

During the start of our Mid-Semester Exams, Aditya was coding for the website during his free time. The original process for the website had a lot of errors, mostly because he wasn't experienced enough to create a responsive web design, completely ignoring any thoughts of implementing any firewalls. After he was done, he asked me (Lionel) to check for any grammarical errors in his paragraphs, as well as write more information to the website. So we co-operated in creating this website, with Aditya focusing on coding the design and creating the script and backend script needed for the website. While I (Lionel) wrote the paragraph's your reading right now!

The process wasn't easy, and they went through lots of trial and errors. At first, we didnt want to use html5 premade code but after noticing that our time in this school is coming to an end. We decided on it and we remade and rewrote the old website with what your currently seeing right now!
